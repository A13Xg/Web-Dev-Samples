FAQ

Everything you need to know before you go.

  • Tickets (digital or print), a valid government-issued ID, sunscreen, comfortable shoes, a reusable water bottle, and a poncho for unpredictable weather. Small blankets and low-back folding chairs are welcome on the lawn areas.
  • Yes! On-site camping is available adjacent to the festival grounds. Options include general tent camping, car camping (vehicle + tent), and premium glamping packages with pre-pitched bell tents and bedding. Camping passes are sold separately from festival tickets.
  • Outside beverages (except factory-sealed water bottles) are not permitted. Our Culinary Village features 50+ food vendors spanning every taste and dietary need, from vegan bowls to wood-fired pizza. Snack bars and craft beverages are available throughout the grounds.
  • Free general parking is available in Lots A–D off Festival Way. ADA-accessible spots are in Lot A, nearest the main entrance. We strongly encourage carpooling. Shuttles run from Sunset Valley Transit Center every 20 minutes, 12PM–3AM each day.
  • All ticket sales are final — no refunds are issued. However, tickets are fully transferable. You can transfer your ticket to another person via the Horizon Fest app up to 24 hours before the event.
  • Horizon Fest is a rain-or-shine event and the show goes on in light rain — we recommend bringing a compact poncho. In the event of severe weather or lightning, all stage performances will pause and attendees will be directed to designated shelter zones on the grounds.
  • We love animals, but only trained service animals with proper documentation are permitted on the festival grounds. Please contact us at info@horizonfest.com if you have any questions regarding service animal access.
  • Yes! Re-entry is allowed each day. Upon exit, present your wristband for a hand stamp. Wristbands are issued at check-in and must be worn at all times. Lost or removed wristbands cannot be replaced without a purchase receipt.
  • Horizon Fest is committed to full accessibility. The grounds include paved accessible pathways, dedicated viewing platforms at every stage, accessible restrooms, and an Accessibility Services tent near the main entrance. Email access@horizonfest.com to arrange support in advance.
