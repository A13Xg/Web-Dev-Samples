Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

For over three decades, Maison Noir has been a sanctuary for those who seek the extraordinary.

Founded by Chef Laurent Dubois in 1987, our restaurant began as a modest 20-seat dining room in the heart of the city. Today, it stands as a testament to unwavering dedication to culinary excellence.

Every dish tells a story—of local farmers who rise before dawn, of time-honored techniques passed through generations, and of bold innovation that pushes boundaries.

Laurent Dubois Executive Chef & Founder
"Cooking is not just about ingredients, techniques, and dishes. It's about harnessing imagination, memories, and emotions."
— Chef Laurent Dubois
